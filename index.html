Muahabaah

Game

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Goal

You are a dog trying to herd sheep into a pen. The sheep will avoid you, breed, and generally make your life a pain in the ass. You have to get a certain number of sheep into the pen before too many die.

Rules

The dog is black, the pen is white, the sheep is blue. If the dog is adjacent from the sheep, the sheep will move away from it. The sheep will also move randomly, cuz it don't give a f&#. Good luck.
